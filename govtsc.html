Pradhan Mantri Janaushadhi Pariyojna

Pradhan Mantri Janaushadhi Pariyojna (PMBJP) was launched by the Department of Pharmaceuticals, Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers, Government of India in November, 2008. Under the scheme dedicated outlets known as Janaushadhi Kendras are opened to provide generic medicines at affordable prices. • The Production Linked Incentive (PLI) scheme for promotion of domestic manufacturing of critical Key Starting Materials (KSMs)/ Drug Intermediates (DIs) and Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) has been notified to boost domestic manufacturing of bulk drugs and reduce import dependence. • Under the scheme, financial incentives will be provided on incremental sales of 41 products given in the guidelines.
